Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Tax Buddy?

Tax Buddy is an automated tool to help individuals and businesses calculate and file their taxes easily.

2. How do I file my income tax?

You can file your income tax through the official Income Tax Portal or using Tax Buddy for faster calculations.

3. What is income tax?

Income tax is a tax imposed on individuals or entities based on their income.

4. What is capital gains tax?

Capital gains tax is a tax on the profit made from selling assets like property, stocks, or bonds.

5. How is income tax calculated?

Income tax is calculated based on your taxable income and applicable tax slabs.

6. Who needs to file income tax returns (ITR)?

Anyone with a taxable income above the basic exemption limit must file an ITR.

7. What are the types of capital gains?

Capital gains are categorized as short-term and long-term, depending on the holding period of the asset.

8. Can I claim deductions while filing my taxes?

Yes, you can claim deductions under sections like 80C, 80D, etc., to reduce your taxable income.

9. How does Tax Buddy help with tax filing?

Tax Buddy simplifies tax filing by calculating your tax liability and helping you file returns directly.

10. Is my data secure with Tax Buddy?

Yes, Tax Buddy uses encryption and other security measures to protect your data.

11. When should I file my tax returns?

Tax returns should be filed before the due date, which is usually July 31st of each assessment year.
